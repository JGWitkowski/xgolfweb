Payment

We accept cash and credit card.

Please note: To play a round of 18 holes it takes an average of 1 hour per person. Please come 10-15 minutes before tee time to pick your course and get set up.

Reservation Changes or Cancellations:

  • Credit card required to reserve all tee times.
  • Cash or gift certificate payment may be used at check-in.
  • No fees charged for cancelling or rescheduling reservations up to 24 hours before the tee time.
  • Booking multiple simulators are subject to our 24-hour cancelation policy.
  • Reservation changes made within 24 hours of a tee time will incur a fee equal to 75% of the simulator rental rate charged to your credit card. If the original reservation can be re-booked, no fee will be charged to your credit card.
  • A no-show will incur the full simulator rental rate charged to your credit card.
